Safety & protection

Why students trust KnightMarket

KnightShield Buyer Protection

Shop with confidence. Every eligible purchase made through KnightMarket is protected by KnightShield Buyer Protection. Payments made outside the app — cash, Venmo, Zelle, Cash App — are not covered.

Protected purchases require payment through KnightMarket.

Secure Escrow Payments

Your money stays protected until the transaction is complete.

  • Buyer pays securely through the app
  • Funds are held in escrow
  • Seller gets paid only after successful delivery or pickup
  • Disputes can be opened for items that aren't as described

QR Code Pickup Verification

For in-person exchanges, sellers receive a unique QR code after a purchase.

  • Inspect the item before accepting it
  • Scan the QR code when satisfied
  • Payment is instantly released to the seller
  • No scan, no payment release

Verified UCF Students Only

Every account must be verified with a UCF email address.

  • Student-only community
  • Reduced risk of fake accounts
  • Greater accountability
  • Safer campus transactions

Trusted Seller Program

Build trust through successful transactions.

  • Reviews only count for purchases completed through KnightMarket
  • Earn Trusted Seller badges
  • Gain visibility in search results
  • Build a verified reputation on campus

Scam Prevention

KnightMarket is designed to keep transactions safe.

  • Contact information is automatically filtered
  • Messaging stays inside the app
  • Exact pickup locations are shared only when appropriate
  • Suspicious activity can be reported and reviewed

Fair Dispute Resolution

If something goes wrong, we're here to help.

  • Buyer protection for eligible purchases
  • Dispute resolution support
  • Protection fund for unresolved cases
  • Dedicated review process for both buyers and sellers
